Our Mission

To provide a platform for music lovers to share their thoughts and feelings about their favourite songs and artists. At our core, we believe that music is a universal language that can bring people together and through this platform, we hope to create a community of music lovers who can share their love of music with others. We also want to give back, and we will be donating a portion of our profits to music education programs in schools and artist programs to protect them from exploitation and current economic climate; helping to ensure that music remains a vital part of our culture.

Our Vision

To create a community of music lovers who can share their love of music with others. This will be achieved through the creation of a platform that allows users to share their thoughts and feelings about their favourite songs and artists. We have a view, about changing the landscape of the music industry, by providing a platform for artists to share their music with the world and connect with their fans. We also want to give back, and we will be donating a portion of our profits to music education programs in schools and artist programs to protect them from exploitation and current economic climate; helping to ensure that music remains a vital part of our culture.

We have detailed our actions to help and extend our mission and vision at our partner link My Mission, My Vision page.

Our Values

  • Respect - for our users, our partners, and our team
  • Integrity - in everything we do
  • Passion - for music and for our community, keeping it alive
  • Quality - in our content and our service
  • Transparency - in our actions and our decisions; especially those in our mission and vision

You can read more about our values on our Values page.
