Our Values

These values are the driving force behind my love, my mind.

What we stand for

  • Respect

    We believe that respect is the foundation of all meaningful interactions. Whether it's engaging with our users, collaborating with our partners, or working within our team, we prioritize treating everyone with dignity and appreciation. By fostering a culture of respect, we create a welcoming and inclusive environment where creativity and collaboration can thrive.

  • Integrity

    This is at the core of everything we do. We are committed to honesty, ethical decision-making, and staying true to our values, even when faced with challenges. Our users and partners trust us because we uphold the highest standards of professionalism and accountability, ensuring that our platform remains a reliable and credible space for music enthusiasts.

  • Passion

    Music is more than just sound—it’s an experience, a culture, and a way of life. We are deeply passionate about music and the community that surrounds it. This passion drives us to celebrate and preserve musical expression, support emerging talent, and provide a platform where music lovers can connect, discover, and share their love for the art.

  • Quality

    Excellence is our standard. Whether it’s the content we create, the services we provide, or the experiences we deliver, we are dedicated to maintaining the highest quality. Our users deserve the best, and we continually strive to improve, innovate, and refine our offerings to ensure we exceed expectations.

  • Transparency

    Openness and honesty guide our actions and decisions. We believe in clear communication, especially when it comes to our mission, vision, and business practices. By maintaining transparency, we build trust with our community, ensuring that our users and partners always understand our goals and the reasoning behind our choices.


Your Rights

At my love, my mind, we are committed to protecting your rights and ensuring a fair and transparent experience on our platform. Below are the key rights you have as a user:

Right to Access

You have the right to access and use our platform in accordance with our terms and conditions. This includes browsing content, engaging with the community, and utilizing the services we provide.

Right to Privacy

Your personal data is yours. We are committed to handling your information responsibly, and you have the right to know how your data is collected, stored, and used. You can request access to your data or ask for it to be deleted at any time.

Right to Expression

We support free and respectful expression. You have the right to share your thoughts, opinions, and creative works, provided they comply with our guidelines and do not harm others.

Right to Security

We take your security seriously. You have the right to a safe browsing experience, and we actively work to protect our users from fraud, harassment, and unauthorized access to their accounts.

Right to Support

If you encounter any issues, you have the right to reach out to our support team for assistance. We are here to help and ensure your experience on our platform is smooth and enjoyable.

Right to Control Your Content

Any content you create and share belongs to you. You have control over your posts, and you can edit or remove them as needed. We respect your ownership and will not use your content without your consent.

Right to Transparency

We are committed to being open about our policies, decisions, and updates. You have the right to clear communication regarding changes to our services, terms, and privacy policies.

By using our platform, you agree to uphold these rights while also respecting the rights of others. If you have any questions or concerns, please contact our support team.
